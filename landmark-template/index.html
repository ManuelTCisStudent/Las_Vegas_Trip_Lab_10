My First Visit

Through my early life, I was aware of Las Vegas as the city to go for gambling related reasons, but nothing else beyond that. So it was quite a thrill to have my family decide to use our summer vacaction to Las Vegas, even if it wasn't sure what would be fun over there as a 12 year old. Likewise, making our way to the city had driven me insane with boredom as desert scenario became all to common on the car ride there. However, once settling in the view of the city and the large sign indicated the city's name, I knew I wouldn't forget my vacation at Las Vegas.

The Circus Circus Hotel

Our first destination for the first half of our vacation would be at the Circus Circus hotel, which even as I child knew it was a place designed to be more child-friendly with its brand. However that didn't stop me from running around the place, looking around the playareas, and watching the various different circus theme attractions catch my eye. Of course, even my parents seemed to be having a blast within this all new and different enviroment. So this hotel is quite a special place in my heart, espcially after we won ourserves a large fluffy plush polar bear doll.

The Famed Excalibur Hotel

Our second hotel place to stay was the Excalibur hotel, which at the time, seemed to deepen my spirits as the overall design of the place was pretty cool, it lacked the colorfull flare of our first hotel. However, while the hotel lacked the excitement of the last place, I did managed to find myself enjoying the place through the better tasting selection of food's present within the hotel and the smaller entertainment center. However most importantly, the overall location of the hotel allowed us to explore a more varied collection of famed destination within the city, and we managed to gather so many different memories from our more laxed way of traveling on foot.

Til Next Time Las Vegas!

Through this history rewind, I discussed my own personal history within the acclaimed city (even if not acclaimed for the best reasons), however despite my well spoken words about the city, I ultimately ended up with my first visit as my seemingly last visit. Back in the day, our family actually the freedom and money to go on this pretty cool trip, but recent years haven't been too kind on our fiancial situation and likewise, we don't have the time to actually freely spend on heading out of state due to school, jobs, and pets. Still it's not like I've completely thrown away the idea of heading once more to the City of Sin, however next time, I'll be sure to continue avoiding the gambling sections in fear of losing any extra money.
